Addressing Your Core Challenges

In the race to tapeout, pre-silicon verification is your final safeguard against costly respins. Yet, most chip failures trace back to missed bugs in verification.

Our mission: close coverage gaps, slash debug cycles, and protect your timeline

Design Closure and Time-to-Market

Our detailed and iterative approach—from synthesis and timing analysis to signoff—minimizes design iterations and ensures a faster, smoother transition from RTL to GDSII. We work collaboratively with your team to identify and resolve potential bottlenecks early in the design process.

Optimizing Power, Performance, and Area

Achieving the right balance in power, performance, and area is a common challenge. Our specialized flow development and rigorous analysis help meet your PPA goals, ensuring that every design decision supports your product’s success

Ensuring Robustness and Signoff Readiness

We’re not just about meeting deadlines; our comprehensive services—from floorplanning to routing and CTS—are engineered to deliver robust, signoff-ready GDSII files. This means fewer surprises during manufacturing and a smoother transition to production

Our Expertise, Your Competitive Edge

Arcana Semiconductor isn’t about flashy promises—we provide proven, practical solutions backed by a track record of successful SoC tapeouts and IP hardening projects at nodes ranging from 16 to 5nm. Our team of industry veterans works side-by-side with you, ensuring that every step of the pre‑silicon process is optimized for quality and efficiency.

Synthesis & Timing Analysis

We leverage advanced synthesis techniques and static timing analysis to create a strong foundation that meets your PPA requirements, reducing the need for costly late-stage revisions

Floorplanning & Feasibility Studies

Our thorough floorplanning process includes power planning and feasibility assessments, ensuring that your layout is optimized for both performance and manufacturability from the outset.

Placement & Clock Tree Synthesis (CTS)

With a keen focus on signal integrity and timing optimization, our team designs efficient placements and clock distribution networks that support your design’s operational goals

Routing & Signoff Preparation

Addressing challenges like electromigration, IR drop, and DRC/LVS early in the process, we deliver clean, reliable routing solutions that streamline the final signoff
