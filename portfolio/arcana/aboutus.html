About Us

ArcanaSemi is a global leader in AI-driven SoC and IP design, delivering end-to-end solutions from microarchitecture to tapeout-ready GDSII. Founded by semiconductor veterans with decades of experience in Silicon Valley and the UK, we specialize in cutting-edge technologies such as RISC-V, Arm-based AI accelerators, and advanced-node designs (5nm, 7nm) for industries ranging from automotive to edge computing and beyond.

Our mission is to empower innovators with first-time-success silicon, combining world-class engineering with scalable offshore design centers (ODCs) in multiple locations. With tens of successful tapeouts and a focus on power, performance, and area (PPA) optimization, we bridge the gap between leading-edge ideas, aggressive design goals, tight project schedules, and market-ready chips
