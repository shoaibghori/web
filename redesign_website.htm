You ask yourself the question of redesigning your website ? Is your site is outdated and difficult to use? Warning, it could mean the end of your reputation to see your business.

As a trader, your business is your livelihood.

Your reputation and credibility have enormous importance. Naturally you want to show everyone that your company is professional and competent . But if your site works poorly or is generally not up, your business and your reputation may suffer considerably. So do not let your website hurt your image. If the telltale signs that it is time to react escape you, here are a few that might just suggest that it is time to invest in a new website.

1 - Your website is not mobile friendly

More than half of the online business is done today on smart phones and tablets .And this percentage is expected to grow in the coming years. Therefore, it is absolutely essential that your website is mobile friendly, that is designed for the mobile. People increasingly tend to seek the services they need when they are already en route. If your website is not visible on smartphone or tablet, or proves unwieldy on these devices, it is clear that many cases may escape. Poor visibility, links difficult to open and distorted images are all valid reasons for someone in need of your skills go do business elsewhere. Therefore, make sure your site is ready for the challenge!

2 - Your site is not optimized SEO

When a customer needs a merchant emergency, it is more than likely he will not waste valuable time watching the websites of dozens of local businesses. It will be much more likely to visit the first name on Google. If the SEO of your business website is not properly optimized, you will soon become invisible to search engines like Google, Yahoo! and Bing. A new site can be built with a SEO strategy in mind . This then translates into better positions in search results, so more inquiries, and more clients final.

3 - It is hard to navigate your site

If potential customers are taking too long to find what they want, there are very likely to see it go to your competitor's site. And with an ancient site, this problem can be tricky to solve. Therefore, the simplest way to ensure that navigation on your website is as user-friendly as possible is to do it again, from the foundation, from scratch, opting for a website specifically designed to make the experience user a good time .

4 - Your site looks tired

A site dated gives the impression to customers that your company is tired and needs a change. This can cause you to lose business opportunities, even if you are the best in your field. Animated gifs, flash intros, and outdated plugins are all warning bells for visitors to your site, which will surely not want to be teleported to the mid-2000s Redesigning your website is graphically emergency . Hold your credibility and reputation by investing in a website that gives your business a modern look while being fast, efficient and functional.

5 - Your site does not offer new content

constantly provide new content is the key for your site to always appear as fresh and exciting. It is also very important for your website to be well positioned in search engines. Redesigning your website will specifically with thought in mind generating new content. This means you'll be able to add blog posts, infographics, e-books, videos, and just about everything you want to make your customer engagement easier. Not only does this improve the performance of your website, but it will also improve your position as market leader. This will also attract visitors looking for additional information.
